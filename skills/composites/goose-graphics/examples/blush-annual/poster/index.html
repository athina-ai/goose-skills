In Review — Five Lessons

5

Tips for building a startup in 2026.

A quiet annual letter to founders shipping into a noisier year. Distilled from twenty-three operators who built something that lasted.

No. 01

Ship fast, learn faster.

Launch in weeks. Iteration speed beats perfect planning — embarrass yourself early.

No. 02

Build AI into the core.

In 2026, AI is infrastructure. Embed from day one — retrofitting costs 3× more.

No. 03

Hire for leverage, not headcount.

Small teams with high-leverage tooling outperform bloated orgs. One 10× hire beats three average ones.

No. 04

Obsess over 10 users before 10,000.

Deep retention with a few beats shallow acquisition at scale.

05

No. 05

Revenue beats runway.

Default to revenue-positive early. Fundraising is a tool, not a strategy.
