Build AI into the core, not the periphery.

The strongest 2026 startups did not bolt AI onto an existing workflow as a marketing line. They rebuilt the workflow around it. Their teams shipped faster because the model was a teammate, not a tool. Their margins held because the model replaced the parts that used to be expensive. The founders who treated AI as core infrastructure compounded; the ones who treated it as a feature decoration did not.
